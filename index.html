    Research

    16S rRNA Sequencing & Analysis of Soil Microbial Communities

    Conducted research under Mrs. Sara Forrester and Mr. Justin Podowski for the Biosciences Division at Argonne National Laboratory. Analyzing soil microbial communities using 16S rRNA sequencing and studying the effects of chemical exposures on bacterial compositions with the goal of establishing correlations between chemical exposure to soil biodiversity.

    Role of Hydrogen Bonds in Electron Transfer Processes of Type-I Copper Proteins

    Conducted research under Dr. Narayanasami Sukumar at Argonne National Laboratory utilizing the Advanced Photon Source (APS). Crystallized Lysozyme and obtained data regarding its structure and bonding utilizing the APS. Performed bioinformatics analysis on this data and that of other carefully chosen copper proteins to understand the relationship between protein function and hydrogen bonding.
